Ballman Beatdown is an Action RPG story-rich/fighting game with a Super Smash Bros style combat system.

You play as stupid and empty-headed Ballman, who believes himself to be the first and only of his kind....But will that all change?!
Join Ballman and his friends Alexander 'Skeeter' Sullivan and Sophia Davis as they go to uncover more about Ballman and his past! Meet villains and heroes alike... and destroy both!

release date: december 32nd 2030
